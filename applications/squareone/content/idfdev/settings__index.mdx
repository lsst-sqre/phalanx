# Account settings

<Lede>Your account settings are available at [https://id-dev.lsst.cloud](https://id-dev.lsst.cloud).</Lede>

## Management tips:

Use the menu in the upper right corner of the [https://id-dev.lsst.cloud](https://id-dev.lsst.cloud) page to access your settings:

- **My Profile.** View your profile information.

- **My Group Memberships.** View and manage your group memberships.

- **Link another account.** Add additional identity providers to your account. Link to a personal account like GitHub or ORCiD in case your institutional login changes.
