# Get help with the Rubin Science Platform

<Lede>Besides the <Link href="/docs">documentation</Link>, you can get help
from Rubin Observatory staff. Here are the ways to ask for help.</Lede>

<Section>
    ## Community forum

    Ask questions and solve problems with the help of Rubin Observatory staff and colleagues in the [Support category of the Rubin Observatory Community forum](https://community.lsst.org/c/support/).

    <CtaLink href="http://community.lsst.org/new-topic?category=support">Create a science support topic in the forum</CtaLink>

    ## Rubin Science Assemblies

    Weekly [Rubin Science Assemblies](https://rubinobservatory.org/for-scientists/events-deadlines/events-rsa) offer presentations, hands-on tutorials, and drop-in office hours to support use of the Rubin data products, services, and tools.

    ## Keeping up to date

    The Rubin Science Platform is constantly improving with exciting new features and bug fixes.
    [*Staying up to date*](https://rsp.lsst.io/guides/life/updates.html) provides tips for following the latest developments to make the most of the platform.

</Section>
